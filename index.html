Questions

What researchers ask us most

Is it allowed to get external help with my MSCA proposal?

Yes. The European Commission has no restrictions on professional writing support for grant applications. Many universities even provide this internally. The intellectual content and scientific vision must be yours — we help you communicate it effectively.

How early should I start before the deadline?

We recommend starting at least 3-4 months before submission. The MSCA call typically closes in September, so reaching out by May or June gives us enough time for thorough revision cycles without pressure.

What if my proposal is not funded?

We offer a discounted resubmission service for returning clients. Many successful applicants are funded on their second or third attempt — evaluator feedback is invaluable, and we help you act on it strategically.

Do you cover all research fields?

We work across all panels — physical sciences, life sciences, social sciences and humanities, engineering. Our team includes specialist reviewers in each domain to ensure your proposal is assessed by someone who understands your field.

Can you help me find a host institution?

While our core service focuses on the proposal itself, we can advise on how to evaluate potential host institutions and what evaluators look for in the fit between researcher and host. For active brokerage we recommend dedicated MSCA matchmaking platforms.
